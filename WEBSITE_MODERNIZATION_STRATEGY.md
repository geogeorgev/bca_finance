## Website Modernization Audit

### Design Assessment
**Modern websites have:**
- ✅ Minimalist, clean layout (less is more)
- ✅ Professional color palette (2-3 colors maximum)
- ✅ Large, readable typography
- ✅ Whitespace (breathing room)
- ✅ High-quality images
- ✅ Consistent branding
- ✅ Clear visual hierarchy
- ✅ Modern buttons and hover effects

### User Experience (UX)
**Modern websites provide:**
- ✅ Clear navigation (visitors know where to go)
- ✅ Fast loading (<3 seconds)
- ✅ Mobile-friendly (works perfectly on phones)
- ✅ Accessibility (works for all users)
- ✅ Clear calls-to-action (CTAs)
- ✅ Easy contact (multiple ways to reach you)
- ✅ Search functionality
- ✅ Related content links

### Content Strategy
**Modern church websites include:**
- ✅ Clear mission statement
- ✅ Service times prominently displayed
- ✅ About the church (your story)
- ✅ Leadership team (build trust)
- ✅ Service descriptions (what happens on Sunday)
- ✅ Event calendar
- ✅ Online giving/donate button
- ✅ Blog/news updates
- ✅ Prayer requests
- ✅ Small groups/ministries
- ✅ Contact/directions

### Technical
**Modern websites are:**
- ✅ Mobile responsive (works on all devices)
- ✅ Fast loading (optimized images)
- ✅ SSL encrypted (HTTPS)
- ✅ SEO optimized
- ✅ Analytics enabled
- ✅ Social media connected
- ✅ Up-to-date software

---

### Area 7: Performance & Speed

**Modern websites load fast:**
- Optimize images (compress without quality loss)
- Minimize apps/widgets
- Use modern browser features
- Lazy load images (load as user scrolls)

**Actions:**
1. Check page load time (Google PageSpeed)
2. Compress large images
3. Remove unused apps/widgets
4. Test on slow internet
5. Monitor performance

**Time:** 2-4 hours
**Tools:** Google PageSpeed Insights, Wix analytics

---

### Area 8: Accessibility (ADA Compliance)

**Modern websites are accessible:**
- Alt text for images
- Proper heading structure
- Good color contrast
- Keyboard navigation
- Mobile friendly
- Clear focus indicators

**Actions:**
1. Add alt text to all images
2. Use proper heading hierarchy
3. Check color contrast
4. Test keyboard navigation
5. Ensure forms are accessible

**Time:** 3-5 hours
**Tools:** WebAIM checker, Wix accessibility features

---
