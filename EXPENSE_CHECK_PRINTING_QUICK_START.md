## 🚀 Quick Start - 2 Minutes

### How to Print a Check

1. **Add Expense**
   - Expense → "+ Add Expense"
   - Fill in all details
   - **Payment Method:** Select "Check"
   - Enter check number

2. **Save Expense**
   - Click "Save Expense"
   - Success message appears

3. **Print Check Dialog**
   - Dialog shows with check details
   - Click "🖨️ Print Check"

4. **Print**
   - Browser print dialog opens
   - Load pre-printed check stock into printer
   - Click "Print"
   - Check prints with all details filled in

## 📐 Check Stock Requirements

**Standard Business Checks:** 8.5" × 3.33"
- Check with stub on left (deposit section)
- Blank area for MICR numbers (pre-printed)
- Pre-printed check layout (optional, this fills in details)

**Alignment Tip:**
Print test on regular paper first to verify alignment with your check stock.

## ✉️ Envelope Printing

### How to Print Envelope
1. Click "✉️ Print Envelope" from dialog
2. Envelope layout opens
3. Load standard #10 envelope in printer
4. Click Print
5. Add or verify addresses as needed

### Standard #10 Envelope
- Size: 9.5" × 4.125"
- Common business envelope
- Shows return address (top left)
- Shows recipient (center)

## ❓ FAQ

### Q: What if check doesn't print correctly?
**A:** Adjust printer margins in print dialog settings and try again. Test on regular paper first.

### Q: Can I reprint a check?
**A:** Yes, the expense stays in the list. You can return to it and use browser print function if needed.

### Q: What stock should I use?
**A:** Use bank-approved check stock compatible with your bank account. Check with your bank for approved stock.

### Q: Do I have to print immediately?
**A:** No, you can skip printing and print later. Just open the expense and print from browser.

### Q: Can I print on regular paper?
**A:** Yes for testing, but actual checks must be on approved check stock for banking purposes.

### Q: What about check design/logo?
**A:** This feature prints on blank areas of pre-designed checks. Your bank likely provides check design.

## 📞 Troubleshooting

### "Print dialog not appearing?"
→ Check that Payment Method = "Check" is selected

### "Check number not showing?"
→ Check stock may have pre-printed areas. Verify positioning.

### "Amount in words looks wrong?"
→ Numbers convert automatically. Verify amount entered is correct.

### "Envelope not printing correctly?"
→ Ensure standard #10 envelope is selected in printer. Adjust margins.

### "Can't find printer?"
→ Select printer in browser print dialog. Ensure printer is connected.
